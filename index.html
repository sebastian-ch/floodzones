Find Your Flood Zone

Flood insurance is federally required for all structures within the Special Flood Hazard Area (SFHA, or the 100-year flood zone) that carry a federally backed mortage.
The flood maps are NOT property or structure specific, regardless of what anyone tells you. This is where much of the confusion arises from. If your lender or insurance company tells you that you are within the SFHA, that is their interpretation of the flood maps. The only way to receive a structure or property specific determination is to apply for a Letter of Map Amendment(LOMA).
